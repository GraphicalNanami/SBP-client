## The Stellar Ambassador Program

The Stellar Ambassador Program supports builders and educators who are passionate about Stellar as they form regional chapters, organize local events, lead educational initiatives, and connect developers, businesses, and users with the resources necessary to build on the network.

To build and serve local communities, ambassadors:

Connect on the Stellar Dev Discord, onboard new members, support projects in their region, and generally create a supportive community

Attend weekly Discord meetings to share thoughts and ideas

Host, help promote, and attend in-person events

Create and translate long-form content and educational resources

Create, share, and engage with Stellar-related social media content

Become verified Stellar Community Fund (SCF) members and join the SCF voting process

Contribute to the monthly ambassador newsletter

Drive the creation of new programs and resources

You can find the regional chapter chat channels in the Stellar Dev Discord, which is also where most chapters host weekly meetings and discuss upcoming initiatives.

Join the Stellar Dev Discord to participate [here](http://discord.gg/stellardev).

Check out upcoming events [here](https://stellarregionalambassadors.notion.site/1452f53cc0b280e595a9c0714b8cb0e3?v=4e994847611a497c8d34423c01d409e0&pvs=25).

Interested in joining a regional chapter? Complete the questionnaire [here](https://stellarregionalambassadors.notion.site/854719d75aa04405a1a2a463b8b7474b?pvs=25#139a44b7b4a64e6b86deaa33e325fbf9).

On this Notion, you can learn about upcoming and past events, see weekly meeting schedules, find regional chapters on social media, read Stellar learning resources in different languages, and find contact information for chapter presidents and leaders.

Objectives:

Educate: Equip communities with the knowledge to build on Stellar, teach developers Rust and other relevant coding languages, and create resources in multiple languages to serve builders all over the world.

Nurture: Foster a vibrant and inclusive Stellar community, welcome and support ecosystem participants of all kinds, and encourage participation in the Stellar Community Fund.

Connect: Help innovators find one another, allow developers to interact with potential users, and share ideas via events and social media to celebrate local activations and contributions

Build: Collaborate to identify local problems, create solutions tailored to address local challenges, and align to drive transformation.
